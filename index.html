Why Johannesburg

Gauteng the city of gold

Johannesburg is known as "The City of Gold" due to the significant gold rush and mining industry that shaped its development and the province of Gauteng, according to Wikipedia. The name Gauteng, meaning "gold place" in Sotho-Tswana, also reflects the province's rich history related to gold mining. The city's location within the Witwatersrand, a mineral-rich area, further solidifies its connection to gold and its nickname.
